Resetting a Parameter Template
==============================

**Scenarios**
-------------

You can reset all parameters in a custom parameter template to their default settings.

Procedure
---------

#. Log in to the management console.
#. Click |image1| in the upper left corner and select a region and project.
#. Click |image2| in the upper left corner of the page and choose **Databases** > **GaussDB**. The GaussDB console is displayed.
#. On the **Parameter Templates** page, click **Custom Templates**. Locate the parameter template and choose **More** > **Reset** in the **Operation** column.
#. Click **Yes**.
